About Free Focus

Free Focus is a non-profit organization with a mission to help non-profits make informed decisions and improve their operations through the use of data. Our goal is to make data accessible, affordable and tailored to each non-profit's specific needs.

We understand that many non-profits lack the resources and expertise to effectively use data to inform their decision-making. That's why we offer data services that include data collection, analysis, and reporting, as well as customized support for each organization's unique needs. Our team of experienced professionals work closely with each non-profit to understand their requirements and provide tailored solutions that will help them make a bigger impact.

At Free Focus, we believe in the power of data to drive positive change in the world. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, along with our passion for using data to make a positive impact, sets us apart as a leader in the non-profit sector. Join us on our mission to help non-profits succeed through the use of data.
